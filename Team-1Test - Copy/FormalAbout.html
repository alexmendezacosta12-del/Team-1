About Us

The story behind Clerks Formal

Crafted with Purpose

Clerks was founded on a simple belief: that every step you take should reflect the standard you hold yourself to. Our formal collection is built for those who understand that dressing well is not vanity — it is respect for the moment, the room, and the people in it.

Each pair in the Clerks Formal line is hand-finished by our craftsmen and carries the Clerks mark — engraved, embossed, or stitched — as a quiet signature of quality. From the boardroom to black-tie, we make shoes that endure.

We source only the finest full-grain leathers, and every sole is Blake-stitched for a sleek profile and long-lasting construction. Because your shoes should work as hard as you do — and look better for it.
